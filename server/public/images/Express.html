What is dap?

Ever go to a new place, like a new school or gym, and struggle to remember the names of everyone you meet? Now, the limits of human memory are a thing of the past! With dap, getting someones name, photo, facebook, and more is as simple as touching phones! Using Near field commuication, you can instatly get a record of everyone you meet, as well as a simple, efficient method of looking them up, wheather through the app itself, or online using our website, dapapp.me!

How to use

Using dap is extremely easy, even with those who dont own the app! Once you download the app and fill in your info, sharing it is as simple as clicking the dap button, and touching phones with your new acquaintance, and your info has instantly been sent to their phone. If they do not have the app, they will be directed to your web-profile where they can view your info, and get a download link to start dapping themselves!
